Avengers: Endgame - Captain Marvel

Avengers: Endgame | VFX Breakdown | DNEG

I had an amazing opportunity to work on Captain Marvel character at DNEG for Endgame. We were entirely replacing her costume with a CG one in the begining of the film. I was the lead modeller on the suit (based on a concept from great Andy Park) and I also modelled her head, which was used in several shots too. At the end I was making several texture and lookdev tweaks.

Apart from Captain Marvel I was working on few other smaller assets (models, textures, lookdev) in the film, like Captain America costume, time suits, time watch, detailing Benatar or Avengers compound.
